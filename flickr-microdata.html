Bakery Photograph

My friend Carey and I took a trip across the border today to Tecate, Mexico. Reason for the trip? Breakfast and bread - both delicious! Mexico is so colorful - and I was grateful I'd brought my nice DSLR. Only brought my f1/8, 50mm, hence the severe DOF on the bakery shots!

Taken with a Kodak V705 DUAL LENS.

Taken on July 21, 2009 at 9am UTC.
